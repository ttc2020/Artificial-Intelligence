Advantages of Artificial Intelligence

Advantages of AI

  1. AI can be used to increase the work efficiency in industries
  2. AI can reduce the errors and imperfections in the work caused by humans
  3. AI can solve complex problem which are not concievable by human mind
  4. AI can be used in daily tasks, like remainders, schedules etc
  5. AI can reduce the cost of training of people and also reduce the operating cost which in turn increases profits
